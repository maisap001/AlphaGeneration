Nutrition Tips

Eat a balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, proteins, and whole grains. Aim to fill half your plate with vegetables and fruits. Stay hydrated and limit processed foods.

Exercise

Regular physical activity is essential for maintaining a healthy body. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week. Choose activities you enjoy, whether it's playing a sport, dancing, or hiking. The more fun it is, the easier it is to stick with.

Common Health Issues

Obesity, diabetes, and heart disease are on the rise due to poor diet and sedentary lifestyles. Stay active and eat healthily to prevent these conditions.
