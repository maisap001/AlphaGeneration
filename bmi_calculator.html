What is BMI?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index, a number calculated from your height and weight. It gives an indication of whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or greater
